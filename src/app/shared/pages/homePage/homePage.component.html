Frequently asked question

  • Is my vote anonymous?

    Yes. The system guarantees anonymity and confidentiality throughout the entire process.

  • What happens if I close the page during voting?

    Your vote will only be saved if you confirm it. If you leave before confirming, it will not be registered.

  • Can I vote more than once?

    No. You can only cast one vote per election, and the system will automatically monitor this.
